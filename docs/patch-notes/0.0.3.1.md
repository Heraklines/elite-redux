# Elite Redux 0.0.3.1

A small follow-up patch - new toys for reading enemy kits, livelier trainers, and a batch of fixes.

## ✨ New Features

- **Enemy innates now unlock as foes level up.** A wild or trainer Pokémon starts with only its first innate active; its **2nd innate unlocks at Lv15** and its **3rd at Lv24**. The in-battle **Info** screen shows exactly which innates are still locked (and at what level), so you can read a foe's full kit and plan around what's actually live.
- **Trainers rotate their teams.** Elite/Hell trainers draw from a larger roster than they field, and now bring a **different random subset each run** instead of the same Pokémon every time.

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

- **Prismatic Fur** (Kecleong) now changes type to resist - or become immune to - an incoming attack **before** it lands, so the type swap actually reduces the damage taken. Previously it changed too late to matter.
- **Aftermath** Pokémon are no longer left invincible. A rare timing case (the holder knocking out the last foe with its own move) could leave an Aftermath holder permanently surviving at 1 HP - fixed.
- Fixed a **battle freeze** that could occur on certain encounters - a stuck weather/effect animation can no longer hang the game.
- The **Info** screen's ability page now correctly greys out and labels innates that aren't active yet (**Locked** / **Disabled**), matching the party Summary page, instead of showing every innate as if it were live.

## ⚙️ Under the Hood

- **Faster updates.** New versions now load right away without needing to clear anything - your saves are never touched.
