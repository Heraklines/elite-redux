# Elite Redux 0.0.3.5

## Bug Fixes

- **Gym Leaders, Elite Four, Champions & evil-team Leaders now appear as bosses (Elite/Hell).** ER's hand-built marquee teams were mapped to trainer types the wave schedule never picked, so boss waves got generic teams. Boss waves now pull from ER's marquee pool, wave-scaled (gym-leader tier early, champion tier late) — still different every battle and every run. (Rivals keep their own path.)
- **Far more trainer variety in Elite/Hell.** A run was only fielding ~19–20 distinct ER teams (some repeated 7–8×). Once a type's fresh trainers run out, the game now pulls from the whole unused ER pool (400+ tier-eligible trainers) before repeating — Elite fields 77 distinct, Hell 96. Strength still scales with the wave.
- **Elite/Hell trainers no longer repeat after a reload.** The "already fought" list is now saved with your run and restored on load (older saves restore to a fresh pool).
- **Mega Evolution fixed across the board.** Most ER megas — and every Redux-custom mega — had no mega form wired up, so they never appeared and players holding the stone couldn't Mega-Evolve. Mega forms were rebuilt from ER's evolution data (186 → 273 of 283), Mega Stones in trainer hands are recognized, and split forms (Mega-X/Y, Primal, Origin) resolve correctly. Restores Redux-custom megas like Mega Redux Reuniclus.
- **No more megas appearing too early in Ace/Elite.** Megas are gated to wave 50+ in Ace and Elite (Hell exempt); rival/boss aces fielded directly as a mega now show their base form before wave 50.
- **Gallantry no longer acts like Sturdy.** It now negates the first instance of damage the holder takes (the hit connects and side-effects still apply, but damage is 0).
- **Wyrm Wind no longer stacks its stat change per hit.** It now raises Speed +1 / lowers Sp. Def −1 once, after all hits, like Scale Shot.
- **Redux-form icons at the top of the battle screen are correct again.** They now fall back to the correct base-form icon instead of showing the wrong Pokémon.
- **Morpeko (Hangry) cleanup.** The Hangry form no longer shows in eggs or starter selection, the "using run away" console spam is fixed, and Two-Faced no longer pops up every turn doing nothing.
- **Infernape (Redux) fixes.** Correct sprite; the junk "Infernape Redux B" placeholder is filtered out; and Long Reach no longer lets Flame Body's non-contact burn proc through.
- **Catastrophe now actually boosts.** In sun, Water moves (and in rain, Fire moves) now cancel the adverse-weather penalty and apply the boost (net ×1.5), like Hydro Steam.
- **Flame Body / Static / Poison Point no longer proc off the wrong things.** They no longer trigger on status moves or on switch-in; the non-contact tier only fires on a damaging attack, and Poison Point is contact-only.
- **Solar Power no longer chips your HP in sun.** The 1/8 HP drain is removed; the Sp. Atk boost in harsh sunlight stays.
- **Calyrex's Ice Rider / Shadow Rider forms now have their innates.** Both riders get their proper innate triple.
- **Challenge Favour no longer farms extra candy from eggs.** Egg-hatch candy keeps only the standard boost, not the run's Favour multiplier (egg shininess is unaffected).
- **Every Redux line is now reachable from eggs.** When a line has no lower Redux to evolve from (e.g. Weavile Redux), the lowest existing Redux form hatches directly at a higher cost reflecting its stage.
- **Redux Pokémon no longer evolve into a Gigantamax/Mega form.** Evolutions never land on a Mega/Primal/Gigantamax/Eternamax form, fixing both the wrong result and the evolution-screen crash.
- **Post-battle freeze fixed (eggs).** A blank/leftover species id could slip into the egg pool and freeze the game after a wild battle; the pool now refuses unregistered species, with a second safety net in the hatch roll.
- **"Wooly Worm" is now an Epic-tier egg** (was banding as Rare).
- **Darmanitan Redux "Bond"/"Blunder" no longer hatch or appear as starters** (battle-only forms, now excluded like Mega/Primal/Hangry).
- **No Turning Back no longer boosts over and over.** Its "+1 all stats below ½ HP" now fires exactly once per battle.
- **Effect Spore & Poison Touch are contact-only again.** Their stray non-contact tier is removed.
- **Purple Haze (Redux Scizor) fires right after the attack.** Its follow-up Poison Gas now resolves immediately, at its intended 20 BP.
- **Devious Shot now actually bleeds.** It inflicts the proper ticking Bleed (4–6 turns), plus its +1 crit stage.
- **Poison vs Flying respects the type chart.** Poison's super-effectiveness vs Flying now multiplies into the rest of the chart (pure Flying = 2×, Ground/Flying = 1×, Steel/Flying = 0×).
- **Dragon Rage deals real damage.** It's now ER's normal 80-BP Dragon attack (full stats/STAB/type-chart/crits), not vanilla's fixed 40.
- **Pokédex luck no longer lies for ER customs.** Luck now reflects your real caught data (hidden until you've actually caught one).
- **Locust Swarm / Hivemind now works — and shows the right sprite.** The Hivemind form is injected, the ability swaps in/out around ¼ HP, and both forms render the correct ER art instead of 404ing.
- **More multi-hit / move-power corrections.** Thunder Shock hits at 80 BP (a stale override pinned it to 40), and Cross Poison is corrected to 40 BP/hit (its 2-hit / high-crit / 10% poison unchanged).
- **Chilling Water inflicts Frostbite, not an Attack drop.** It's a 75-BP Water move with a 30% chance to inflict Frostbite.
- **Multi-Headed now hits once per head — not always 3×.** Head counts come from ER's own two-/three-headed flags.
- **ER statuses are now cured by everything that should cure them.** Frostbite, Bleed and Fear are cleared by Lum Berry, Full Heal/Full Restore, Heal Bell/Aromatherapy, Rest, and Natural Cure / Shed Skin / Hydration / Healer.
- **Aftermath explodes before the holder can sneak in another move.** The explosion now fires immediately on faint.
- **Thundercall triggers off Thunder Shock again.** A re-entry guard that swallowed the holder's own Thunder Shock is fixed.
- **Pokédex candy costs match the real (reworked) prices.** The Pokédex and starter screen now share one cost formula.
- **Double battles show both foes' move effectiveness** while choosing a move.
- **More Epic-tier eggs.** Tornadus (Therian) and the Typhlosion custom "Lumbering Sloth Engulfed" are now Epic-tier.
- **Ability Randomizer now works on fused Pokémon.** Fused mons now honor the active-ability override so the reroll takes effect.
- **Many-form families no longer flood egg hatches.** Each form's hatch weight is divided by its family size (Unown, Arceus, Silvally, Pikachu caps, Furfrou, Castform/Rotom, etc.).
- **Unown's "Revelation" Schooling works end-to-end** — schools in on entry above ¼ HP, reverts at/below ¼ HP and on faint, with the correct sprite, abilities, and innates.
- **Redux Kakuna → Mega Beedrill blocked.** Evolving onto a held-stone Mega/Gigantamax form is prevented.

## New Features

- **Damage Calc panel in the fight menu.** While choosing a move, press R (controller: RB; mobile: the on-screen R button) to cycle the right-hand panel to a Damage Calc view — the move's damage as a percentage range of the target's current HP, with a possible/guaranteed-KO flag. Locked until you hold a Damage Calculator (master-tier item); status moves show "—", and it says "no target" if nothing's on the field.
