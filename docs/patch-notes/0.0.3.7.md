# Elite Redux 0.0.3.7

## Abilities
- **Eerie Fog reworked.** Fog no longer drops accuracy. Instead, at the end of each turn it **saps one stage off every raised stat** of non-Ghost/non-Psychic Pokémon, and **Ghost-types take reduced damage** while it's up. Ghost/Psychic mons keep their boosts. (Ominous Wind also hits for **2× power** in fog — see Moves.)
- **Normalize now ignores resistances.** A Normalize move that's normally resisted (e.g. Tackle vs Steelix) now lands for **neutral** damage. Immunities still apply — a Normal move still does nothing to a Ghost.
- **Gifted Mind nulls the holder's Psychic weakness.** A Fighting-type with Gifted Mind (and Galarian Articuno's "Null Psychic Weakness") now takes **neutral** damage from Psychic instead of super-effective.
- **Fire Aspect burns on every hit.** The holder's attacks — even non-Fire ones — now inflict **burn**.
- **Pressure clears the foe's stat boosts on entry.** Switching a Pressure mon in now snaps the opposing Pokémon's **positive** stat stages back to zero.
- **Molten Down no longer over-applies super-effective.** It used to treat *every* Rock-type hit as 2× and ignore the target's other type; damage is now correct (e.g. neutral vs Water/Rock Relicanth).

## Moves
- **Triple Axel (and the other Triple-* moves) hit and ramp correctly** — three strikes, each stronger than the last, instead of fizzling.
- **Aura Force hits Ghosts** — the Fighting-type move now connects with Ghost-types **and** is super-effective (it used to do nothing, like a normal Fighting move into a Ghost).
- **Berry Smash now eats one of the user's berries**, as its description says — gaining that berry's effect. If you're holding several, it picks one at random. (It dealt damage but ate nothing before.)
- **Ominous Wind deals 2× in fog** (base 60 → 120), matching the Eerie Fog rework.
- **Dragon Rage's move info now reads 80 BP** (it showed "1" before — a leftover from its old fixed-damage version). The damage was already correct; only the displayed number was wrong.

## Forms & Evolution
- **Redux lines keep their Redux form through their FINAL evolution.** A Redux-form mon (e.g. Redux Kadabra) now evolves into the **Redux** version of its next stage — with the right sprite, typing, **and its Redux learnset** (so lines that should get Ice moves now do) — instead of reverting to the plain form.

## Rewards
- **Innate-gated items stay out of the reward pool until you can use them.** Items that only help a *locked* innate (e.g. a Mystical Rock for an un-unlocked Seed Sower) no longer clutter your shop/reward choices.

## UI
- **The Damage Calculator now shows your 5th move.** Mons with the rogue-tier extra move slot had their last move cut off the Damage Calculator panel; all five now display.

## Starters
- **Squawkabilly Green form costs the same as the default form** (the form mismatch that charged 5 vs 2 is fixed).
