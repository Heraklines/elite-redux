# Elite Redux — 0.0.3 Patch Notes

A big balance, faithfulness, and quality-of-life pass. Highlights below.

## ✨ New Features

- **Choose your evolution path.** When a Pokémon can evolve more than one way at once (e.g. Magikarp → Gyarados **or** Gyaradeath), you now pick which one.
- **Egg "Discard Eggs" button.** New option in the Egg Gacha to clear your egg backlog (with a confirmation) if you ever get stuck.
- **Egg hatching is now batched.** Huge egg backlogs hatch **1000 at a time** over successive waves instead of all at once — no more freezing on a giant pile of eggs.
- **New Elite/Hell final boss.** On Elite and Hell difficulty the classic final boss is now a two-phase **Cascoon → Primal Cascoon** fight (a drop-in for Eternatus → Eternamax). Ace keeps Eternatus.
- **Custom-mon starter costs re-tiered.** New Pokémon and Redux forms now have sensible point costs (legendaries 8-12, Redux forms 3-4, etc.). Battle-only forms that should only appear through an ability/item (Palafin Hero, Wishiwashi School, Minior cores, Vivillon patterns, Morpeko Hangry, …) are no longer hatchable or directly selectable.

## ⚖️ Balance & Faithfulness

- **Anger Point** now raises Attack by +1 on **any** damaging hit (physical or special) and **maxes** Attack on a critical hit — matching ER.
- **Sky Attack** now raises the user's Attack on its charge turn.
- **Bubble Beam** is now a 25-power Water pulse that hits **2-5 times** (Mega Launcher boosted), not the old 65-power single hit.
- **Dragon Rush** and every other ER-rewritten move now display the correct ER description (e.g. Dragon Rush's recoil + flinch).
- **Big Pecks** and every other ER-rewritten **ability** now show the correct ER text instead of the vanilla description.
- **Paldea Tauros** breeds (Combat / Blaze / Aqua) now get their proper distinct abilities and passives instead of vanilla ones.
- **Elite Mode trainer density** no longer drops off mid-run — the gauntlet stays dense through the whole run.
- Mega/alternate-form **typing fixes** (Mega Beedrill, Aegislash Blade, Lycanroc Midday, etc.).

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

- **Non-contact moves no longer trigger contact abilities or take contact damage reduction.** Every custom physical move (beams, blasts, throws like **Primal Beam**) now correctly respects whether it makes contact — so Fluffy/Static/Rough Skin behave properly.
- **Aftermath** (and Balloon Bomb / Victory Bomb) can no longer be dodged by finishing the holder with a **multi-hit** move.
- **Mega forms can be reverted** again from the Pokémon menu — including custom and Redux megas.
- **Disguise** on custom fusions with no busted form (e.g. **Mimikyu Rayquaza**) no longer blocks every hit forever; it only nullifies a hit when the holder can actually break into another form. Real Mimikyu / Eiscue are unchanged.
- Visible **status icons** for the new statuses (Bleed / Frostbite / Fear).
- Megas no longer leak into eggs or random spawns.
- **Air Blower** now actually sets Tailwind on entry.
- Discord & GitHub links now only show on the starter-select screen.
