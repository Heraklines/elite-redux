# Pokerogue Redux 0.0.4.2

## New: Biome Markets

Every 10 waves now opens a real shop instead of an empty boss reward screen, a
full 4x4 grid of 16 items. Each biome runs its own kind of store: a desert
caravan, a volcano forge, a graveyard shaman, a slum black market, a space
observatory, and so on. The shopkeeper fits the place too (a baker in town, a
fisherman on the docks, a hex maniac in the graveyard, a Clefairy out in space,
a Kecleon down in the caves), and the backdrop is the actual biome you're
standing in.

Prices scale with each item's rarity and how deep you are, and every biome
discounts different things, so it's worth a look at what's cheap where. Rarer
items stock fewer copies and can sell out. No healing items here, that stays
with the normal between-wave shop. Buy as much as you can afford, press B to
leave.

## Moves

- Cross Chop is two 40 BP hits with a high crit ratio now, not a single 100 BP swing.
- Mind Reader is its real ER move: a priority protect that dodges the turn's attacks and drops the attacker's Special Defense on contact. It can fail if used in a row.
- Trapping moves (Bind, Wrap, Fire Spin, and friends) chip 1/6 of max HP each turn. They were doing nothing.
- More moves pick up their ability boosts straight from the dex: Keen Edge, Mega Launcher, Iron Fist, Strong Jaw, Mighty Horn, Striker, and Archer.
- Dire Claw and the ER custom blades count as slicing moves, so they get Sharpness, Sweeping Edge, and Keen Edge.

## Abilities

- Restraining Order (Gooschase) and Chuckster force the attacker out, not the holder.
- Ability Capsule no longer locks you into an ability that becomes a duplicate innate after evolution.

## Pokemon

- Clobbopus evolves into Grapploct at level 24, no hidden "must know Taunt" requirement.
- Candy and passive unlocks now pool to one shared total per evolution line. Lines with a baby form (Pichu, Cleffa, Magby, and the like) were splitting candy across stages and showing paid passives as Locked after evolving. Existing saves fix themselves on load and no candy is lost.
- Move lists no longer show duplicates. Drifloon, for example, listed Psycho Shift twice when picking moves.

## Battles & Modes

- Every enemy spawn now respects the wave power ceiling on all difficulties, closing the last gaps that let ER customs and overtuned mons into early Youngster and Ace runs (the wave 15 Iron Scythe report).

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed a crash when opening the Abilities screen after certain evolutions (Gimmighoul).
- Fixed a freeze when cancelling an evolution (Eevee).
- Unown Revelation, a battle only form, no longer hatches from eggs.

## Eggs & Collection

- More Redux and ER custom Pokemon got full egg move sets.
