# Pokerogue Redux 0.0.5.2

## New

- Reorder your party out of battle. In Check Team (pause menu or the reward
  shop), pick a Pokemon, choose Move, then pick another slot to swap. The order
  sticks for the run. Useful for relics and anything slot based.
- Notifications inbox. A mail icon at the top right of the title screen (press Up
  from the menu to reach it). When your ghost fights another player it shows up
  here with both teams and how many it downed. Toggle under Settings, General,
  Ghost Battle Notifications.

## Battles

- Throat Chop now actually stops sound moves, including one locked in the same
  turn it lands.
- Festivities now works both ways: sound moves count as dances and dances count
  as sound.
- High Tide and Glacial Rage trigger once per move instead of once per hit.
- Chuckster only forces the target out on the first contact hit of a move.
- Many ER abilities corrected to match the 2.65 dex: offensive and defensive
  procs, stat effects, trapping, priority gates, and several blanket-effect bugs.
- Various Pokemon received ability updates.
- Fixed a crash at the start of a turn when a field slot was empty.

## Pokemon and Megas

- Mega and primal forms show their real sprite again in battle, the Pokedex, and
  the starter screen, instead of the base Pokemon.
- Mega and primal forms use their correct abilities and moves from the dex.
- Base forms show their ER base stats in the Pokedex (some were showing vanilla
  stats).
- Wild Pokemon no longer randomly de-evolve.

## Saves and Quality of Life

- Relics now persist across save and reload.
- Save and Quit no longer freezes when your storage is full or a server request
  hangs.
- A fainted on-field Pokemon is no longer wrongly flagged as ineligible for a
  challenge.
- Learner's Shroom is no longer used up when you back out of the move screen.
- Moving to a new biome on the World Map now applies that biome's weather and
  terrain.

## Misc

- The title screen now shows the mod version and build id (instead of the
  upstream version), so you can confirm you are on the latest build before
  reporting a bug.
