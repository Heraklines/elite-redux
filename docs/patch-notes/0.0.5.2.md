# Pokerogue Redux 0.0.4.3

## Move Your Pokemon Around

You can now reorder your party outside of battle. Open Check Team (from the
pause menu, or in the reward shop between waves), highlight a Pokemon, pick
Move, then pick the slot to swap it with. Handy for relics and anything that
cares about party position. The new order sticks for the rest of the run.

## Notifications

There is now a mail icon at the top right of the title screen (press Up from the
top menu option to highlight it, then confirm to open it). It is the home for
game news, and the first thing it tracks is your ghost: when your ghost fights
another player you get a note saying who, how many of their team it downed, and
a side by side picture of both teams. A dot and a count show when something is
unread. You can Mark all read or Clear all.

You can turn it off under Settings, General, Ghost Battle Notifications. With it
off nothing is fetched. More kinds of notices will use this same inbox later.

## Battles

- Throat Chop now actually stops sound moves. A sound move locked in the same
  turn the user is throat-chopped is cancelled, not just blocked next turn.

## Also

- Mega sprites show their real artwork again in battle, the Pokedex, and the
  starter screen. Some were falling back to the base Pokemon's sprite.
- The title screen now prints the build id next to the version, so you can
  confirm you are on the latest build before reporting a bug.
