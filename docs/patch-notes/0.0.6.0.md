# PokeRogue Redux v0.0.6.0

This was our most technically difficult update yet. Thank you for playing, testing, and reporting issues.

## The headline

- Every Elite Redux ability, move, and battle mechanic is now implemented.
- Vanilla PokeRogue abilities and moves that were missing or only partially implemented are now complete too.

## New battle systems

- **Triple Battles:** full positioning, adjacency, and triple-targeting mechanics, a Triples Only mode, triple-specific achievements, and a new Triples Inferno challenge.
- **Native multi-typing:** Pokemon can naturally have any number of types. Primal Regigigas, for example, has six native types. Pokemon that previously needed an ability for a bonus type are now natively multi-typed and received new innates.
- **Battle backgrounds:** every battle background now has new artwork, except the construction site, which is still being completed.

## Items, shinies, and achievements

- **27 competitive held items:** Expert Belt, Covert Cloak, Red Card, Eject Button, Heavy-Duty Boots, Air Balloon, Safety Goggles, Clear Amulet, Ability Shield, Booster Energy, Throat Spray, Blunder Policy, Punching Glove, Muscle Band, Wise Glasses, Zoom Lens, Metronome, Eject Pack, Shed Shell, Adrenaline Orb, Room Service, Iron Ball, Float Stone, Sticky Barb, Smoke Ball, Mental Herb, and Utility Umbrella.
- **Shiny Lab:** more than double the palettes and surface effects, plus entirely new effect categories.
- **115 new achievements** across the new systems and content.

## Showdown and Tournaments preview

Showdown and Tournament Mode are included in this update, but are **temporarily disabled** while their final live-service work is completed. Their title-screen entries remain visible and will tell you when they are unavailable.

When enabled, Showdown provides real level-100 1v1 PvP, account-synced team presets, a complete set editor, Pokemon Showdown text import/export, fair-fight rules, friendly matches, optional server-side stakes, and your Ghost Trainer presentation.

Tournament Mode adds seeded single-elimination brackets for up to 64 players, asynchronous round windows, deadline/no-show resolution, a live bracket board, Ghost Trainer icons, match-ready notifications, and prize-only tournament matches.

## New Pokemon and forms

- More than 30 new and returning Pokemon and forms, including five returning from older Elite Redux versions.
- New egg-pool and evolution content throughout.
- **Partner Eevee Omniform:** one team slot can transform between Partner Eevee and all 11 Eeveelutions before a matching damaging move resolves. Every form carries its own moveset, PP, ability, and three innates. Forms can be chained turn after turn and revert on fainting or at the end of trainer battles.

## Quality of life

- Reset button.
- Safer cloud saves with protection against corrupted data and bad overwrites.
- iOS startup improvements.
- Asset-load retries and deferred audio loading.
- Multiple softlock fixes and many smaller improvements.

## Co-op

Full two-player shared runs are still in development and need a little more time. Co-op remains available only in staging while we finish it properly.
