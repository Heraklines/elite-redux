## Accepted Gaps

Gaps below are tracked here per the Phase B plan, with remediation owned by Phase C asset work.

### Path-layout mismatch (`<variant>/<slug>.png` vs upstream `<slug>/<variant>.png`)

**Status:** systemic — affects the bulk of "completely missing" entries above.

The manifest emits paths in the form `assets/images/pokemon/elite-redux/<variant>/<slug>.png`
(e.g. `front/bulbasaur.png`, `icons/bulbasaur.png`). However the `er:fetch-sprites` script mirrors
upstream's directory structure verbatim, which is `<slug>/<variant>.png`
(e.g. `bulbasaur/front.png`, `bulbasaur/icon.png`). The sprites ARE on disk — just under different paths.

**Remediation (Phase C):** either (a) re-layout the mirrored sprites into the manifest-expected
structure at fetch time, or (b) regenerate the manifest builder to emit paths matching the upstream
layout. Option (b) is cheaper and avoids divergence from upstream naming.

### No shiny PNGs upstream

**Status:** structural — every species's `shinyFront` + `shinyBack` will be missing.

Upstream Elite-Redux ships shiny variants as palette files (`shiny.pal`), not separate PNGs.
There are no `shiny/front/<slug>.png` or `shiny/back/<slug>.png` sources to mirror.

**Remediation (Phase C):** runtime palette-swap support (preferred), OR a one-time offline render
that produces shiny PNGs from `normal.pal` + `shiny.pal` + base sprite. Until then, ER customs will
show their non-shiny sprite even when the player rolls a shiny.
